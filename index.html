About Me

Welcome to my website! My name is Charlotte Kalutycz and I am a recent graduate of the Department of Computing Science at the University of Alberta. I'm a bilingual (English/French) Canadian citizen originally from Surrey, BC and currently located in Edmonton, AB. I first began coding when I was 15 years old, and I've been in love with programming ever since. I have a passion for developing creative solutions to unique problems, and a broad toolkit of technical knowledge to help me along the way. I'm proficient in:

  • Python,
  • Java,
  • C/C++,
  • Javascript,
  • HTML/CSS,

and I'm eager to learn more languages as my career progresses. My ultimate goal is to contribute to a software company which truly improves the lives of its users. There are countless applications that have helped me progress through my education, work experience, and personal life, and I hope to one day design software which can likewise help others succeed.
